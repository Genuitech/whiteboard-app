# WISP Addendum — Secure Client Intake Portal

This addendum documents safeguards for collection of taxpayer and banking information through the secure intake portal.

## Covered data
- SSN/TIN
- Date of birth
- Bank routing/account numbers
- Tax and identity documents

## Safeguards
- Encryption in transit and at rest
- Field-level encryption for high-risk identifiers
- Access limited by role and business need
- MFA and session controls for staff access
- Audit logs for read/write/export actions

## Operational controls
- Prohibit sensitive data over standard email/SMS
- Time-limited intake links
- Data minimization and retention limits
- Incident response with client notification workflow as required by law

## Review cadence
- Quarterly control review
- Annual policy and training refresh
